Linus Torvalds

Who is Linus Torvalds?

Linus Torvalds is a Finnish-American software engineer best known for creating the Linux kernel and Git. Born in 1969, he developed the Linux kernel in 1991 while studying at the University of Helsinki. His work led to the rise of Linux-based operating systems, which power everything from servers to smartphones.
